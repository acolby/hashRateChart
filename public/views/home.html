should i mine bitcoin?

In order to answer this question we must first determine the cost-revenue structure of the Bitcoin market. A Bitcoin mining rig follows a classic input-output profit model. Each rig includes both fixed and variable costs while offering a steady stream of revenue. Depending on the type of miner, total costs over a two-year period can be calculated exactly; however, the biggest unknown when it comes to mining bitcoin is determining the network hash rate. The network hash-rate essentially tells you how much of the bitcoin mining pie you will receive from each block. To provide investors some insight into the question of mining feasibility, we've taken the last year of hash-rate data and extrapolated out two years into the future using a 3rd order polynomial.
